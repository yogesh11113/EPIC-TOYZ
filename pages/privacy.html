Welcome to Epic Toyz. This Privacy Policy explains how Epic Toyz ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website, place orders, or interact with us via WhatsApp, email, or other communication channels. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. This information falls into two categories:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number when you place an order, create an account, or contact us.
  • Delivery Information: Shipping address, city, state, and pin code required to fulfill your orders.
  • Payment Information: UPI transaction references, bank transfer details (we do NOT store complete card or bank account numbers).
  • Communications: Messages, queries, and feedback you share with us via WhatsApp, email, or our contact form.
  • Account Information: Username and password if you create an account on our website.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, links clicked, browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • IP Address: Approximate geographic location, and internet service provider information.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: Session data, shopping cart contents, and user preferences stored locally.

2How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order Processing: To process, confirm, and fulfill your orders, and to arrange shipping and delivery.
  • Customer Communication: To respond to your inquiries, provide order updates, and offer after-sales support via WhatsApp, phone, and email.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, and to provide personalized shopping experiences.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, identify popular products, and improve our website and services.
  • Marketing (with consent): To send you promotional offers, new product announcements, and exclusive deals — only if you have opted in. You can opt out at any time.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations, including those related to e-commerce and consumer protection.

3Information Sharing

We value your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Shipping & Logistics Partners: We share your name, address, and phone number with courier partners (Delhivery, BlueDart, DTDC) solely for delivery purposes.
  • Payment Processors: Payment transactions are processed through secure UPI infrastructure (NPCI). We may share minimal details as required by payment processors.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority in India.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
We will never sell your personal data to third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies. Your information is used exclusively to serve you better.

4Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Secure cloud database storage with access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited employee access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • No storage of complete payment card or bank account numbers on our servers

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized use of your account.

5Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core website functionality, including maintaining your login session and shopping cart.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve it.
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your cart items and wishlist between sessions.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

6Your Rights

As a user of Epic Toyz, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time by replying "STOP" or contacting us directly.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format where technically feasible.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the legal basis.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at epictoyz.in@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

7Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at epictoyz.in@gmail.com and we will delete such information promptly.

For RC car purchases intended for children (which is perfectly fine!), orders should be placed by a parent or adult guardian who provides their own contact information for the transaction.
