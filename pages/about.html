Our Journey

Born from a Passion for RC Cars

Epic Toyz was founded in 2022 by a group of die-hard RC car enthusiasts who were tired of overpriced imports, poor after-sales support, and a lack of reliable options in the Indian market. We saw the gap — and we filled it.

What started as a small operation with a handful of curated products has grown into one of India's most trusted RC car destinations. We source directly from top global brands including Traxxas, HPI Racing, Arrma, Team Associated, and more — ensuring you always get genuine products at competitive prices.

Our mission is simple: make the world-class RC experience accessible to every Indian enthusiast, whether you're a curious beginner picking up your first car or a seasoned racer chasing podium finishes.

Our Mission & Vision

Two guiding principles that shape every decision we make at Epic Toyz.

🎯

Our Mission

To democratize the RC car hobby in India by offering a curated selection of premium, genuine products backed by knowledgeable support and exceptional after-sales service — making world-class RC experiences accessible to everyone.

  • Genuine products with manufacturer warranty
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden costs
  • Expert guidance for every buyer
  • Lightning-fast pan-India delivery
🔭

Our Vision

To become the undisputed #1 RC car ecosystem in India — not just a store, but a community where enthusiasts connect, learn, race, and grow together. We envision a future where RC racing is a mainstream hobby across every Indian city.

  • Build India's largest RC community
  • Partner with top global RC brands
  • Host RC racing events across India
  • Empower local RC clubs and racers
