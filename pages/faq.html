How do I choose between brushed and brushless motors?
Products
+

This is one of the most common questions we get. Here's a clear breakdown:

  • Brushed motors — More affordable, simpler to maintain, lower top speeds (20-40 km/h). Great for beginners, kids, and casual drivers. If your RC car wears out the brushes, they're cheap to replace.
  • Brushless motors — Higher performance, faster speeds (50-100+ km/h), more efficient, longer lifespan. Ideal for serious hobbyists and racers. Costlier but worth it for performance enthusiasts.

Our recommendation: Start with brushed if you're new to the hobby or buying for a child. Upgrade to brushless once you've built experience and want more speed and performance.
